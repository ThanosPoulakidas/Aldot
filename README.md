# Aldot

Aldot is a display typeface designed by **Thanos Poulakidas**. The project explores geometric forms and contemporary letter construction while maintaining readability and a distinctive visual identity.

## About

Aldot is conceived as a versatile type family suitable for a wide range of applications. Its balanced proportions, moderate contrast, and carefully crafted details allow it to perform exceptionally well in long-form reading, making it ideal for books, editorial design, and extensive text settings. At the same time, its elegant presence and distinctive personality make it equally effective in titles, headings, and other display applications.

Every element of Aldot has been designed to achieve harmony between tradition and contemporary expression. Its letterforms preserve the warmth and rhythm associated with classical serif typefaces while incorporating refined adjustments that enhance legibility and visual consistency across different sizes and uses. Special emphasis on the Greek script ensures a cohesive relationship between the Latin and Greek alphabets, offering a unified typographic voice that feels both familiar and renewed. In addition to its standard character set, Aldot includes old-style figures, whose varying heights and proportions integrate naturally with running text, enhancing the texture and elegance of extended reading. The typeface also features a second set of alternative Greek lowercase characters, offering designers greater flexibility and allowing for different stylistic nuances within the Greek script.

By blending old-style warmth with transitional precision, Aldot presents a contemporary serif that balances heritage and innovation. Equally comfortable in extended texts, books, and titles, it offers a timeless typographic foundation with a distinctive freshness, particularly evident in its treatment of the Greek alphabet. Through the inclusion of old-style numerals and alternative Greek lowercase forms, Aldot provides additional typographic richness, making it a versatile tool for contemporary editorial and display design alike.

The project is developed as an open-source font family and is licensed under the SIL Open Font License v1.1.

## Character Set

Current support includes:

* **GF Latin Core and Plus**
* **GF Greek Core and Plus**
* **GF Cyrillic Core**
* Basic punctuation
* Numerals
* Symbols
* Alternative Greek lowercase forms

Additional language support and glyph coverage may be expanded in future releases.
